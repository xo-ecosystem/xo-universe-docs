# Seven Pillars

The XO ecosystem is guided by seven foundational pillars. Together they define the trust continuum and explain how different parts of the system work together.

!!! tip
    For the functional system view behind these pillars, also see the [Architecture](architecture.md) and [XO Universe Map](xo-universe-map.md) pages.

## 1. Bridge

**Role:** connects Web2, Web3, and real-world assets into one flow.

**Core value:** connectivity and interoperability.

**Use case:** a physical object or citizen-facing asset can link to its digital verification layer.

## 2. Ledger

**Role:** preserves an immutable record of actions, ownership, value flows, and continuity.

**Core value:** transparency and accountability.

**Use case:** public records for sealed events, ownership history, or verified actions.

## 3. Vault

**Role:** stores, seals, and signs assets, credentials, and trust-critical data.

**Core value:** security and sovereignty.

**Use case:** storing important keys, signing content, or anchoring a trust event.

## 4. Pulse

**Role:** publishes the heartbeat of the ecosystem through stories, updates, signals, and visible movement.

**Core value:** awareness and responsiveness.

**Use case:** publishing a new artifact, a system event, or a public milestone.

## 5. Seals

**Role:** marks authenticity across products, artifacts, and narratives.

**Core value:** verification and trust.

**Use case:** a digital or physical item carries a recognizable proof of authenticity.

## 6. Exchange

**Role:** coordinates value, knowledge, and interaction across the ecosystem.

**Core value:** collaboration and transfer.

**Use case:** communities, contributors, or systems exchange verified value or access.

## 7. Traits

**Role:** gives identity, artifacts, and experiences adaptive properties.

**Core value:** identity and playful utility.

**Use case:** an artifact or avatar gains additional properties through interaction.

## Utilities around the pillars

In addition to the seven pillars, XO also depends on supporting utilities that help activate the system:

- **Drops** as the delivery and activation layer
- **Digest** as the reporting and summary layer
- **Agent** as the workflow and coordination layer

Together, the pillars and utilities form a modular system that can scale from storytelling and collectibles to broader trust infrastructure.

!!! note
    The pillars describe the conceptual foundation of XO. The public docs are gradually connecting those concepts to concrete modules, domains, and activation flows.
